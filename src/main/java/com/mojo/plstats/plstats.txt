
Player class
Fields:
       name (cannot be null or empty string, first + last name)
       age (cannot be negative, must be above the age of 14)
       position (only GK, CB, LB, RB, CM, DM, AM, LW, RW, CF allowed)
       club (Only clubs in the premier league allowed, players must have a club)
       shirt number (between 1 and 99)
       no. of goals (cannot be negative)
       no. of assists (cannot be negative)
       games played (cannot be negative)
       Clean sheets (only for GK, LB, RB and CB)
       Height (in cm, )
       Nationality
       Preferred foot (either L or R)
       Market value (in Dollars)
       isAvailable (true or false)

Methods:
        searchPlayer() - by name
        filterPlayers() - by club, age, shirt number, height, preferred foot, nationality
        comparePlayers()
        displayInfo() -
        addPlayer()
        removePlayer()
        transferPlayer()
        updateGoals()
        updateAssists()
